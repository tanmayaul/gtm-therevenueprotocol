How it works

From strategy to booked meetings in 6 steps

Here is exactly what happens after you sign up.

01
Learn

We dig into your offer, your best customers, and who you want to reach. We need to know your business well enough to speak for you.

02

Separate domains, email setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), warmup systems, and calendar links. This is the behind-the-scenes work that keeps you out of spam.

We pull lists of real companies and real people from multiple sources. Every contact matches the industry, size, and job titles you told us to go after.

As data comes in, we change what is not working and double down on what is. We talk through the results with you on regular check-in calls.

FAQ

Common questions

How long until I start seeing meetings?

+
Everything will be launched in 14 days. Meetings start getting booked on that 14th day. The first deal can realistically close within the week after that.

What is required from me?

+
Everything is done for you. Just take the calls. We handle the rest. After a one-hour kickoff, most clients spend less than 30 minutes a week on anything campaign-related.

How do you make sure emails do not land in spam?

+
We send from 15+ dedicated domains, never your main one. Every domain is warmed for 6 weeks before it sends a single message. SPF, DKIM, and DMARC are configured on every domain. Deliverability is monitored daily and we rotate domains automatically if any one starts to flag.

What if I don't get results?

+
You don't pay. Our model is performance-based -- you only pay when qualified prospects actually book and show up to meetings. If we don't deliver, you don't owe us anything. Our incentives are fully aligned with yours.

What if a meeting isn't qualified or doesn't show up?

+
If a call doesn't show up or is unqualified, you aren't billed for it. Simple as that.

What do you mean by qualified?

+
We'll talk to you on the call, and "qualified" will mean a meeting with the job titles you say you want to talk to, at companies you say you want to talk to. You define what qualified looks like -- we deliver it.

How are you different from other agencies?

+
We use a multi-channel approach -- email, LinkedIn, and calling running at the same time. We send more volume while keeping every message personalized. And you only pay on performance. No retainers, no management fees.

How is this different from hiring an SDR?

+
An SDR costs $60K to $90K loaded, takes 3 to 6 months to ramp, manages one channel, and produces a fraction of the volume. We give you a full team -- strategist, copywriter, list builder, infrastructure manager -- on day one for less than the cost of one SDR.

Do you offer any guarantees?

+
Yes. After we go through the strategy call and we know your specific numbers, we have guarantees we can put in place. We'll discuss details on the call.

What CRM do you integrate with?

+
HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Close, and Attio out of the box. Anything else, we can sync via Zapier or webhook.
